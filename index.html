Shoot Geese!



Don't shoot Eagles!

Shoot enough Geese to advance the round!
If a Goose makes it to the edge of the screen, you get a strike. If you shoot an Eagle, you get a strike. The game ends when you get 3 strikes.
Shooting takes Ammo.
Press the R key to reload.
If you run out of ammo, it will reload automatically.
